Beet Smoothie

Description

This delicious beet smoothie is a gorgeous color and naturally sweet thanks to beets, pineapple, and banana.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium beets, scrubbed
  • 1 green apple
  • 1 avocado, peeled and pitted
  • 3/4 cup fresh pineapple chunks
  • 1 banana, peeled
  • ice cubes

Steps:

  1. Juice beets and green apples, including peels in juicer.
  2. Combine apple-beet juice, avocado, pineapple, banana, and ice in a high-speed blender; blend until smooth.
